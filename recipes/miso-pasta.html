Five-Ingredient Creamy Miso Pasta

Miso Pasta!!

  • Kosher salt
  • 16 ounces spaghetti, linguine or bucatini
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons white (or red) miso
  • 4 ounces Parmesan, finely grated (1 packed cup)
  • Kizami nori, furikake or thinly sliced seaweed snacks, for garnish (optional)
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook, stirring occasionally, until al dente. Reserve 1½ cups pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  2. Add the butter, miso and 1¼ cups pasta water to the pot and whisk over medium heat until miso breaks down and liquid is uniform, 1 to 2 minutes. Add the pasta and Parmesan and cook, stirring vigorously with tongs until cheese is melted and sauce emulsifies. (The key word here is “vigorously.” To achieve a silky smooth pasta instead of a gloppy one, put your cacio e pepe skills to work.)
  3. Divide among shallow bowls and sprinkle with nori or furikake, if using.
